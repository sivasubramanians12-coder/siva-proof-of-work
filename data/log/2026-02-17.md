# CoachOS — first live session with real client data

CoachOS ran its first real pre-session prep today. The coach fed it a client name, the upcoming session topic, and the system pulled relevant notes from Obsidian + Airtable, cross-referenced with Pinecone embeddings, and drafted:

- A session brief with relevant history
- Three suggested frameworks to draw on
- Proposed follow-up questions

The coach reviewed, made two small edits, and used it as-is. That's the workflow — nothing goes to the client without human review.

The approval gate was the key design decision. Without it, the system would be a liability. With it, it's leverage.

**Key learning:** The value isn't AI replacing the coach. It's AI handling the admin so the coach can focus on the room.
