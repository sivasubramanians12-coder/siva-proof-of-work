# HD Hire — voice screening live

Integrated ElevenLabs voice screening into the HD Hire pipeline today. Here's how the full flow works now:

1. Application submitted
2. Claude scores the application against the role criteria (instant)
3. If above threshold → candidate gets a Cal.com scheduling link for async voice interview
4. Candidate records a 5-minute voice response to 4 questions
5. Groq Whisper transcribes
6. Claude scores the transcript
7. Summary lands in the hiring dashboard

End-to-end: under 24 hours from application to screened candidate in dashboard. No human involved until the decision point.

For a 30-person company where the hiring manager is also running the business, this is meaningful. Good candidates don't go cold waiting for someone to have bandwidth.

**What remains:** Testing across edge cases — candidates with accents, slow internet, mobile-only users.
