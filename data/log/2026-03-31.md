title: Procurement visibility at a glance — Daily Review MIS dashboards for Warehouse and Procurement
tags: [ops, product, ux]
---

Today's work centred on making procurement and warehouse operations visible in real time. Two new Daily Review tabs were added to the HIllarysOPs-OS dashboard — one for the Warehouse team and one for Procurement — each surfacing a live MIS view that previously required digging through individual PO records. The warehouse tab displays current order and inventory status at a glance; the procurement tab brings overdue POs, pending approvals, and key procurement KPIs into a single view.

The business problem this solves is familiar: operations managers spend the first hour of every morning reconstructing what happened the day before. A dedicated Daily Review tab eliminates that. It turns a reactive morning ritual into a five-minute scan, freeing the team to act rather than audit.

On the UX side, the Daily Review tab received deliberate styling — blue colour coding and a clean badge design — to visually separate it from the transactional tabs. Table rows in the Procurement Daily Review are now clickable and open the Edit PO form directly, cutting the steps needed to action a flagged item. PO forms also gained a hide button to reduce visual clutter when forms aren't needed.

The pattern being built here — MIS dashboard layered on top of operational data, accessible in the same tool the team already uses — is a repeatable module. Warehouse done. Procurement done. Sales pipeline is next.
