The single most productive infrastructure day since the Barley stack launched. Six distinct milestones shipped: CONSTITUTION.md deployed as the governing document for the full agent team; Missions defined and live; Security hardened to 100; agent Skills upgraded across the board; MarketerBot activated (completing the 6-agent team: Barley, Coffee, Coder, Designer, Todo, Marketer); and Orchestration Platform Sprint 1 shipped as the coordination layer for multi-agent task delegation and handoff. CoffeeBot Research v3.0 was also delivered — the most capable research output to date.

The Command Center — the biggest single infrastructure build to date — shipped across three versions earlier in the day. V1 brought agent lifecycle management (spawn, monitor, kill) and a live activity feed. V2 added per-session and aggregate cost tracking, plus a conversation viewer. V3 completed the build with workspace backup/restore, config history tracking, and a mobile-responsive PWA interface. The Command Center solves a real problem: as the agent team runs across Hunter Douglas ops, Spice Lab, and advisory projects, flying blind is a liability. The control plane makes the stack observable and auditable.

The business problem behind today's full-stack upgrade is straightforward. A team of AI agents without a constitution, without defined missions, and without a coordination layer is a collection of tools — not an operating system. CONSTITUTION.md sets the rules every agent operates under. Missions give the team a north star. The Orchestration Platform Sprint 1 gives those agents a proper way to hand off work, escalate blockers, and report back. MarketerBot closes the loop: every build now has a path from commit to post without Siva manually drafting content.

Key decisions made today: mobile-first with PWA for the Command Center (agent status needs to be accessible in the field, not just at the desk); CONSTITUTION.md as a hard-referenced document read at every session start; Orchestration Platform Sprint 1 as the foundation for all future pipeline-based work. CoffeeBot v3.0 research quality to be benchmarked for future research task delegation. The stack is now fully constitutionalised, fully observable, and fully staffed.
