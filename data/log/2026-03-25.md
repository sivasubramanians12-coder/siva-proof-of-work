title: Closing the Loop on Payment — Final Collection Now Visible in CX Workflow
tags: [ops, product, ux]
---

One of the last visible gaps in the Hillary's Ops OS was that the CX team had no dedicated surface to track final payment collection. Orders would reach the installation stage and payment follow-up would fall into email threads and memory — the classic ops smell. Today that gap was closed: a **Collect Final Payment tab** was added to the CX view, giving the team a dedicated workflow step for chasing and confirming final payments post-installation.

Alongside the new tab, a **bug in the Confirmed & Paid status** was fixed. The status wasn't reflecting correctly, which meant ops visibility on closed orders was unreliable — you couldn't trust the dashboard to tell you what was actually paid. That's now corrected.

The combined effect: CX staff can see exactly which orders need final payment action, and the Confirmed & Paid count in the dashboard is trustworthy. Less chasing across tools, fewer orders slipping through without payment closure.

This is the kind of change that's small in code but meaningful in operations — when the workflow matches the actual job to be done, teams stop building workarounds and start using the system.
