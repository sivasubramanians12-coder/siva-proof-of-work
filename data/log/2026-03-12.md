Two systems running in parallel. Vayu shipped v2.0.0 today — audio engine wired in, 4 new breathing techniques, session FSM fixes, and a full origami UI redesign across every component. Hillary's Ops OS added rework pipeline visibility and invoice/advance payment infrastructure.

**Vayu — v2.0.0 + Origami Redesign**

The audio engine is now live: phase-specific tones (inhale / exhale), selectable soundscapes, and volume controls. The session FSM was rearchitected — phase timing was unstable because the timer was in the `tickPhase` dependency array, causing drift over long sessions. Removed. Fixed. Stable.

New techniques added: Bellows Breath (Bhastrika) for energising, diaphragmatic breathing, Extended Exhale, and Wim Hof with an explicit safety warning for hyperventilation risk. That brings the library to 12+ techniques across 5 intent categories.

The origami UI redesign shipped in the same session. The design principle: every UI surface should feel like washi paper — fold shadows replace all visible borders, Instrument Serif for headings, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono 300 for timers. Breath-speed easing (`cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`) on every transition. The redesign touched every component in the app.

**Hillary's Ops OS — Rework Pipeline & Payment Infrastructure**

Added rework pipeline visibility to the CX dashboard — partial orders with rework status, filter by month and owner. The advance payment system was rebuilt: 50% gate with initial invoice tracking, tranche management (CX can remove tranches 2 and 3), and regression prevention for cleared orders. Internal vs external installer classification added to the Check Measure assignment form.
