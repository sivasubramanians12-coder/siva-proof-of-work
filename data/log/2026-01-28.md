# Geneva pipeline — Telegram bot shipped

The Telegram bot for Geneva is live. Field reps update the pipeline by messaging a bot — no app to install, no new credentials to remember, no training session required.

Commands are simple:
- `/update [client] [stage] [notes]`
- `/pipeline` — shows their own pipeline
- `/nudge` — sends a reminder to a stalled deal

Updates flow into Supabase in real time. Leadership dashboards reflect current state. The consolidation step that used to take hours — now it doesn't exist.

The adoption lesson: the best tool is the one that lives where your team already is. Hunter Douglas field reps are on WhatsApp and Telegram all day. Meeting them there, not asking them to open a new app, was the unlock.

**Next for Geneva:** Automated weekly digest to leadership, pulled from live pipeline data.
